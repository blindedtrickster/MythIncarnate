Hey all,

I've read pages and pages of forum threads and lots of different explanations of how damage is calculated, but they are inconsistent and I can't get any of them to explain even simply observable data from the game.

Let's take the first combat you'll come across. These stats are cobbled together from various places over the last couple of weeks but I can't find the references now I'm afraid.

Randi: Str 15, Agi 15, Con 13, Int 5, Wis 5
Rusty Sword: Str 3, Hit 75
Overalls: Def 3, Eva 37

Rabite: Str 3, Agi 1, Con ???, Int 1, Wis 1

Now, with a regular hit, Randi does about 18 damage to the Rabite, and the Rabite does about 4 damage to Randi. I've not seen a single explanation of the formulae that spits out those numbers. Almost all the data I've seen seems to completely ignore the damage mobs do to players; it's all player focussed and doesn't seem to cover the other direction.

All versions agree that your base attack is Str + Weapon Str, and your base defence is Con + Armour Def. Some then involve some more complicated multiplication, where some say just subtract one from the other.

I'm just trying to understand the inner workings. Any help appreciated; if you have an explanation or a reference to any other material I might not have seen that would be great. Thanks.

Here are the base formulas from looking at the code in the game:

Note: GameFAQs censor is being dumb for some reason saying that s*** is somehow uncensored, I'm guessing this has something to do with "hit rate" being included. I adjusted it so that wherever it says "hit rate", there's asterisks to avoid it.

Physical Damages

h** rate = Agility / 4 + weapon h** rate
Attack = Strength + weapon's strength
Evade rate = Agility / 4 + armour's evade rate
Magic evade = Wisdom / 4 + armour's magic evade
Defense = Constitution + armour's defense
Magic defense = Wisdom + armour's magic defense
Critical rate = Weapon skill*3 + weapon's crit rate, halved for fist, sword, axe and spear

A character can't be hit if he or the attacker has no HP left, or if he has a barrel.
Attack succeeds if h** rate is greater than (1+Random)*Evade/2.
Force = Attack*(1+(Charge level+Mana Sword Power)/4). If the attacker is small, force is always 1.
If h** rate is greater than Random*101, Power = (Mana power squared+Force)*(1+Random/16), otherwise Power = h** rate*Force/100.
If evade rate is greater than Random*100, DefPower = Defense+Random*Defense/16, otherwise DefPower = Evade*Defense/80
Critical hits are possible when fully recovered, or with moon energy. These double the attack power.
If target is sleeping, the critical rate doubles. The woman's rate is then increased by 5 and the man and sprite's h** rates are increased by 10. If the result is then greater than Random*256, the hit will be critical.
If the attacker is recovering, Power becomes (1-Frames left/((99-Agility)/2+80))*(Power/2).
Damage equals Power - DefPower. The target is woken up. Status is only inflicted if damage was done and target is not protected against anything it would be inflicted with.


Magic Damages

If magic evade rate is greater than Random*101, DefPower = MagicDef+Random*MagicDef/16, otherwise DefPower = MagicEva*MagicDef/80
Accuracy is equal to wisdom for the woman's spells, and intelligence for the sprite's spells
h** rate = Accuracy/4 + spell h** rate
Force = (Accuracy+Spell's strength)*(1+Spell level/2).
If h** rate is greater than Random*101, Power = (Mana power squared+Force)*(1+Random/16), otherwise Power = h** rate*Force/100.
If target is strong against the spell, attack power is halved, and if it's weak against the spell, attack power is doubled. Everyone is weak against any spell that has positive effects. The power is then divided by the number of targets.
Damage equals Power - DefPower, but not less than 1.

DefPower = MagicDef+Random*MagicDef/16, otherwise DefPower = MagicEva*MagicDef/80
Accuracy is equal to wisdom for the woman's spells, and intelligence for the sprite's spells
h** rate = Accuracy/4 + spell h** rate
Force = (Accuracy+Spell's strength)*(1+Spell level/2).
Power = (Mana power squared+Force)*(1+Random/16), otherwise Power = h** rate*Force/100.
2x or 0.5x
Damage equals Power - DefPower, but not less than 1.

Thanks. I'd read this information before. It seems to express player->mob damage relatively well but is useless for mob->player unless I'm missing something fundamental.

To take the Randi and Rabite stats above (and ignoring magic and crits):
Randi's hit = (15 / 4) + 75 = 78
Randi's attack = 15 + 3 = 18
Randi's evade = (15 / 4) + 37 = 40
Randi's defense = 13 + 3 = 16

Rabite's hit = 1 / 4 + 0 = 0 (but i assume mob hit rates are 100% or otherwise hardcoded)
Rabite's attack = 3 + 0 = 3
Rabite's evade = 1 / 4 + 0 = 0
Rabite's defense = can't find Con data anywhere, assume 0.

So, when Randi hits a Rabite:
Randi's force = 18 * (1 + ((0+0)/4)) = 18
Randi's power for most common case = 78 * 18 / 100 = 14
Rabite's defpower all cases = 0 * 0 / 80 = 0
Therefore damage = 14 - 0 = 14, which is about right. I've definitely seen 14 damage done, though I think there must be some further randomisation.

But when a Rabite hits Randi:
Rabite's force = 3 * (1 + ((0+0)/4)) = 3
Rabite's power, albeit with lots of assumptions = 3
Randi's defpower in most common case = 40 * 16 / 80 = 8
Therefore damage = 3 - 8 = -5, which is obviously wrong. I have never seen a Rabite heal Randi =)

Am I missing something? Again, any help appreciated. I'm trying to reimplement the combat system for a sort of a brief homage to SoM in a game I'm writing.

Your leaving out the fact that monsters have weapons too. A rabite for example as a weapon with an attack power of 7. There's also a random variance applied to attack damage.

Also enemies don't have CON scores.

Couple of corrections to what Yoda posted:
Critical Hit Rate is halved for Bow, Boomerang & Javelin.
Purim gets +10% bonus to critical rate. Randi & Popoie get +5%.

Saber spells grant a damage bonus equal to Power / (10-ElementalLevel).
